1. Product Overview

Field Staff is a white-labeled workforce operations module designed to connect field attendance, HR controls, and payroll preparation in one workflow.

Core Outcomes

  • Capture verifiable attendance with geolocation data.
  • Standardize HR operations through one workspace.
  • Move from attendance to payrun with reporting clarity.

Best Fit Use Cases

  • Field service operations
  • Construction and industrial teams
  • Distributed mobile workforce management

2. Feature Set

Area What It Does Business Impact
Attendance Clock in/out with GPS coordinates, notes, and attendance ledger history. Reduces time ambiguity and supports accountability in field operations.
Employee Pay Setup Stores payroll profile data (rates, allowances, deductions, statutory values). Improves payroll consistency and reduces manual recalculation.
Shift Scheduling Creates shift templates and distributes shifts by staff/department/date range. Improves workforce planning and attendance alignment.
Manual Attendance HR can apply controlled attendance adjustments when needed. Handles real-world exceptions without breaking payroll cycles.
Leave Tracking Records leave entries and status changes (Pending/Approved/Rejected). Keeps scheduling and payroll context synchronized.
Reporting & Payrun Attendance reports + CSV export + payrun statement generation. Supports management visibility and payroll cycle readiness.
Project Assignment Assigns projects to one or multiple staff with date ranges and status tracking. Improves supervisor-level resource allocation.

3. Role and Access Control

The module enforces server-side access checks and renders only permitted workspace tabs.

  • Capabilities: view_own, view, edit, create
  • Project Assignment access uses strict role-ID allowlist (not title matching)
  • Admin panel allows explicit role-ID updates and displays current user role context

5. Payroll Rules Built In

  • Weekly regular hours cap: 44.00
  • Overtime multiplier: 1.5x
  • NIB split: 5.5% employee / 6.5% employer
  • NHIP split: 3.0% employee / 3.0% employer
  • NHIP monthly ceiling reference: 7800.00
  • Flexible additions/deductions supported via EAV payroll attributes.
