5. Objection Handling Cheatsheet

"Will this fit our current workflow?"

Yes. The module mirrors standard attendance, scheduling, leave, and payroll-prep flow in one interface.

"How secure is supervisor access?"

Project Assignment access uses strict explicit role-ID allowlist with server-side checks.

"Can we export data for finance?"

Yes. Attendance and operations reports are exportable in CSV for downstream workflows.

"What if exceptions happen in the field?"

Manual attendance and leave workflows cover operational exceptions with controlled updates.
